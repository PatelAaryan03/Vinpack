Our Products

We offer a comprehensive range of industrial packaging solutions designed to meet diverse industry needs. Each product is manufactured with precision and quality in mind.

HDPE/PP Woven Fabrics

High-density polyethylene and polypropylene woven fabrics engineered for exceptional strength and durability. Ideal for manufacturing sacks, tarpaulins, and industrial covers.

  • UV resistant
  • Waterproof options
  • Custom widths available

FIBC / Jumbo Bags

Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers designed for safe and efficient bulk material handling. Available in various capacities and configurations.

  • 500kg to 2000kg capacity
  • Food-grade options
  • Custom printing available

BOPP Bags

Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene bags with high-quality printing and lamination. Perfect for retail packaging with excellent visual appeal.

  • High-quality printing
  • Moisture barrier
  • Custom sizes and designs

Woven Sacks

Reliable and cost-effective woven sacks for agricultural products, seeds, grains, fertilizers, and industrial powders.

  • Multiple sizes
  • Laminated options
  • Reusable designs

Multifilament Yarns

High-tenacity multifilament yarns manufactured to exacting standards for superior fabric production and performance.

  • Various deniers
  • Consistent quality
  • Bulk supply available

Custom Solutions

Need a specialized packaging solution? We work closely with clients to develop custom products tailored to specific requirements.
