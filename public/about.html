About Vinpack

30+ Years of Excellence in Industrial Packaging

Our Story

Established in 1992, Vinpack has been a trusted name in the field of HDPE/PP woven bags & fabrics and BOPP bags. With over three decades of experience, we have grown from a small manufacturing unit into a reliable packaging partner for businesses across industries.

At Vinpack, we believe that packaging is more than protection – it's about trust. Every bag we manufacture is designed to safeguard your products with strength, durability, and consistency. From agriculture and chemicals to food and industrial goods, our packaging solutions are tailored to meet diverse requirements.

With years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for excellence, innovation, and reliability. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities ensure consistent quality across all our products.
